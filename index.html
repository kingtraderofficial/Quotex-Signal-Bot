Trading Privacy & Rules

  • 1. Capital Exposure: Market trading contains significant financial risk. Only trade with surplus funds. High-frequency 1m trading is volatile and can lead to rapid capital loss.
  • 2. The 35 Strategy: It is highly recommended to use a standard trade amount of $35 or 3% of your balance. Consistency in trade size is the key to surviving long-term market swings.
  • 3. Execution Timing: Our AI signals are calibrated for 1-minute expiry. For maximum precision, execute the trade exactly at the 00-second mark when a new candle opens on your broker.
  • 4. Technical Cross-Check: Never ignore the Technical Analysis Meter inside the pair box. If the meter shows "NEUTRAL," it is strictly advised to skip that signal regardless of the AI output.
  • 5. Martingale Limits: Use Martingale Level 1 (M1) only. If an M1 trade results in a loss, stop immediately for that pair. Over-leveraging through multiple martingales will blow your account.
  • 6. News Avoidance: Do not use the bot 15 minutes before and after "High Impact" news. Economic data releases can override all technical indicators and AI predictions instantly.
  • 7. Session Discipline: Trade only during active market sessions (London or New York). Avoid low-volume periods where price movements are erratic and unpredictable for AI models.
  • 8. Withdrawal Rules: Once you hit 10% profit of your daily starting balance, stop trading. Greed is the biggest enemy in binary options; secure your daily gains in your bank.
  • 9. Loss Management: If you encounter 3 losses in a single session, turn off the bot. The market conditions are not favoring the strategy; wait for the next session to recover slowly.
  • 10. Hardware & Network: Ensure you are using a device with 4G/5G or stable Wi-Fi. A slight lag in your internet connection can result in a different entry point, turning a win into a loss.
  • 11. AI Limitations: The bot uses real-time API data from Binance and Technical Consensus. While highly accurate, it is not 100% perfect. Use it as a confirmation tool for your analysis.
  • 12. Disclaimer: King Trader provides analytical signals only. Any financial profit or loss incurred is the sole responsibility of the user. Use the tool with extreme caution.
