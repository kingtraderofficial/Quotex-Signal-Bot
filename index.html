TRADING PRIVACY & RULES

  • 1. Risk Control: Binary trading involves high risk. Never invest money that you cannot afford to lose completely. We recommend using only 2-5% of your total balance.
  • 2. Amount Setup: For best results, use a fixed amount of 35 USDT or as per your target. Avoid emotional trading and high stake increases after a win.
  • 3. Signal Strategy: These signals are based on 1-Minute expiry. Wait for the exact starting second of the new candle to place your trade for maximum accuracy.
  • 4. Consensus: Always check the Technical Meter provided in the box. If the meter is neutral, skip that signal to protect your capital from market volatility.
  • 5. Martingale: We suggest using only 1-step Martingale (M1) if the first signal fails. Do not go beyond M1 as it can lead to heavy losses in bad market conditions.
  • 6. News Protection: Strictly avoid trading during high-impact news (Red Folder). Check the economic calendar before starting your trading session for the day.
  • 7. Market Selection: Real market pairs are more stable than OTC. Try to trade when major exchanges (London/New York) are open for more reliable price movements.
  • 8. Profit Target: Set a daily profit target of 10-15%. Once achieved, close the bot and withdraw your profits to stay disciplined and consistent in long run.
  • 9. Loss Limit: If you face 3 consecutive losses, stop immediately. The market may not be suitable for the strategy at that moment. Come back after a few hours.
  • 10. Internet Stability: Ensure a high-speed internet connection. Even a 1-second delay in signal execution can result in a loss due to the fast nature of 1m trades.
  • 11. Manual Confirmation: Use your own basic price action knowledge alongside the bot. Combined analysis always gives a higher win rate than relying on AI alone.
  • 12. Responsibility: King Trader provides tools for analysis. We are not responsible for any financial loss. You trade at your own discretion and responsibility.
