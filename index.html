  • The $35 Rule: Standardize your trades at $35 or a fixed percentage. Never chase losses by increasing amounts randomly. Discipline is the only way to profit.
  • Signal Execution: Execute the trade at the very first second (00s) of the new candle. A delay of even 2 seconds can turn a win into a loss in 1m trading.
  • Meter Confirmation: Check the "Technical Analysis" meter in the box. If it points to Neutral, avoid that pair. Only trade when the meter aligns with the AI signal.
  • Martingale Strategy: Only 1-step Martingale (M1) is allowed. If M1 loses, stop trading that pair immediately. Over-martingale will lead to total account blow-out.
  • News Impact: Do not trade during high-impact news releases (Red Folder). News volatility ignores all technical indicators and can crash your strategy instantly.
  • Trading Sessions: Best results are found during the London and New York sessions. Avoid trading in low-volume periods like the Asian session for better accuracy.

  • Loss Limit: If you experience 3 consecutive losses, turn off the bot. The market cycle is not favoring the strategy; wait for the next session to recover.
